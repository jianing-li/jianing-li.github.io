I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Peking University in Computer Science, working in the <a href="https://www.pkuml.org/:Multimedia Learning Group" target="_blank">Multimedia Learning Group</a> (MLG) since 2017, and I am advised by Pro. <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=fn6hJx0AAAAJ&hl=zh-CN:Yonghong Tian" target="_blank">Yonghong Tian</a> and Pro. <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=knvEK4AAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN:Tiejun Huang" target="_blank">Tiejun Huang</a>.

My research is in computer vision and machine learning, with focuses in <a href="https://github.com/uzh-rpg/event-based_vision_resources" target="_blank">event-based vision</a>, neuromorphic engineering and point proprocess learning theory.
